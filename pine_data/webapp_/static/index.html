Replay Workspace

  • Choose the recording root from `Recording root`.
  • Click an episode in `Episode list` to load its metadata and streams.
  • Use `Prev`, `Play`, `Next`, or drag the slider to inspect the trajectory.
  • Switch `Camera view` to compare saved RGB or depth streams.
  • Read `Episode metadata` and `Signals and arrays` to verify what was recorded.
  • Leave `Auto-refresh` on if you want newly saved episodes to appear automatically.

Live Workspace

Recorder Control And Monitoring

Live Workspace

  • Check the source badges first to see whether each input is `Disconnected`, `Connected`, or `Streaming`.
  • Watch the two RGB preview windows to confirm the cameras are live.
  • Press `Initialize` to start or reconnect the SpaceMouse teleop + recorder pipeline. Keyboard: `i`.
  • Press `Reset` to move the arm to the configured home position. Keyboard: `Ctrl+r`.
  • Teleoperate the arm only after the robot and cameras look healthy in the status area.
  • Press `Start episode` to begin a new recording using the current instruction text. Keyboard: `c`.
  • Press `Label subtask` whenever you want to mark a subtask boundary inside the current episode. Keyboard: `l`.
  • Press `Stop and save` to finish the episode and write data to disk. Keyboard: `s`.
  • Use `Delete last` only when you want to remove the most recently saved episode. Keyboard: `d`.
  • Use `Shutdown` to close the tmux backend session and stop the recorder cleanly. Keyboard: `q`.
  • Read the status box at the bottom for tmux logs, save path, and runtime errors.
